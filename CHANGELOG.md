### Features
- **Project Management**: `aiops init`, `aiops config`, `aiops status`
- **Infrastructure Operations**: `aiops scan`, `aiops deploy`, `aiops monitor`
- **Playbook Management**: `aiops playbook list/run/create`
- **Template System**: `aiops template apply/plan/destroy`
- **Logging & Debugging**: `aiops logs`, `aiops debug`
- **Multi-Environment**: Development, staging, production deployments
- **Plugin System**: Extensible architecture for custom detectors and actions

- Package manager support (Homebrew, Chocolatey)
